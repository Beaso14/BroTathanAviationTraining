Our Services

Laser Scanning

Laser scanners capture the entire part geometry for inspection or reverse engineering purposes, covering both freeform surfaces and geometric features. Laser scanners are used with CMMs, articulated arms, optical CMMs or even robots. The resulting digital copy forms the backbone of a powerful digital inspection process that streamlines inspection processes, reduces time to market and cuts development costs. 3D Laser Scanning accelerates design-through-manufacturing by providing full geometry feedback

Reverse Engineering

Reverse Engineering has become a standard procedure for engineers in a wide range of markets. The technique can be used in product development, production engineering, cost down programmes and refurbishing of components. In situations where the engineer has only 2D data or no drawings at all, we will scan the component and post-production of a polygon model will produce a workable solid parametric CAD file with design intent.The file can then be used to modify or manufacture the components. STL files can also be produced to allow the production of rapid prototypes and prototype castings.

Damage Assessment

The process of damage assessment is time consuming. Often the inspection takes a considerable amount of time due to the manual effort and manual inspection methods that is common practise in the industry. The manual skillset is becoming ever more scarce resulting in longer lead times to achieving accurate damage assessment. The results incostly delays, inaccurate reporting and increased costs. Using the latest equipment and software reduces the delay, saves time, gives more accurate date and allows for detailed complex repair engineering solutions to be provided quicker.
