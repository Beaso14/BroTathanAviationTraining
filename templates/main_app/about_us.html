Bro Tathan Aviation Training Ltd

Based in the South of Wales, at BTAT we provide EASA Part 147 and Part 145 training courses.

Whether face to face in a classroom, practical training on live aircraft, course examinations or remote training due to Covid restrictions, we have a range of courses to suit your needs.

Our courses on Airbus and Boeing aircraft are EASA 147.00128 and 147.0123 approved.

As well as our Part 147 training, we provide bespoke courses from Internal Auditor and Personal Development to Inspection Management and Quality Management

We are also offering remote training when COVID restrictions apply.
